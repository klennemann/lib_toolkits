Home Energy and Water Saving Toolkits

Community Action Center

San Mateo County libraries are proud to offer Home Energy and Water Saving Toolkits for residents to check out at no cost. Each toolkit includes items that residents may keep and install in their homes, such as LED lightbulbs, weatherstripping, faucet aerators and more.

This space is for you to see what actions your neighbors and community in San Mateo County are taking to save water and energy. Once you're completed working through the toolkit, add what actions you take in the toolkits using the button below.

The larger the word, the higher the number of people who have tried or are doing this action. If a phrase is capitalized, clicking on it will open a link to more information on that action. Try it out!
