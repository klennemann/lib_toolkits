Check-It-Out! Home Energy & Water Saving Toolkit

The Check-It-Out! Home Energy & Water Saving Toolkit is a resource available in libraries in San Mateo County. It can be checked out just like a library book, and can be used by homeowners and renters alike.

This toolkit provides free tools and resources so residents can improve their home's energy and water efficiency. Users can keep supplies such as faucet aerators, low-flow showerheads, an LED lightbulb, weatherstripping, and borrow equipment like fridge thermometers and drip gauges to measure home energy and water usage.
